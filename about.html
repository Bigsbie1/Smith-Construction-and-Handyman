About Me – Joshua Wayne Smith

My name is Joshua Wayne Smith, and I bring over 30 years of experience in the construction and handyman industry. Throughout my career, I have built a reputation for delivering high-quality workmanship, attention to detail, and dependable service across a wide range of residential and commercial projects.

From home repairs to full renovations, I approach each project with dedication, integrity, and a commitment to excellence. My goal is always the same: to exceed expectations and ensure complete satisfaction — every time.
